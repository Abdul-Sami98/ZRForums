Type of Emails Can You Send

At ZR Forums, you can send different types of emails. They can be divided into 4 categories:

Newsletters

A newsletter can provide information about your products and services and encourage your target group to make a purchase. Sending newsletters regularly helps in generating sales and upsells of goods and services.

Automatic Email Campaigns

With an automatic email campaign, you send emails to recipients based on their interests. The email is tailored to the stage the recipient is in. For example, abandoned carts, birthday events, welcome campaigns, etc.

Transactional Emails

With transactional emails, you can notify your potential customers to sign up or make an online purchase. Example: Purchase confirmation or event registration confirmation.

Service Emails

The purpose of service emails is to keep the customers informed. For example, an email marketing real estate company can send an email if the company policies have changed or if they want to share important information about the company.
